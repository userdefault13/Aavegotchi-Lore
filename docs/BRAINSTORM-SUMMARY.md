## Confusion Clarifications

### Common Misunderstandings Addressed

**Aagents ≠ Aadepts**
- **Aagents** = 2020-2021 real-world Discord community program (not lore)
- **Aadepts** = The Nine ancient Gotchis who sacrificed themselves (main lore)
- Initial searches found "Aagents" (from Aavegotchi wiki mission program)
- Clarified distinction in all documentation

**Burn Address vs. Nether Realm**
- Burn Address = gateway/portal (0x000...dead)
- Nether Realm = entire dimension beyond it
- Gotchiverse = homeland built within Nether Realm

**Lickquidators: Creators or Enemies?**
- Both! They liquidate positions (creating Gotchis indirectly)
- But also hunt and consume Gotchis for yield
- Complex relationship is intentional
