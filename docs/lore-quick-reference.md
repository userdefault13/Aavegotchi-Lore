# Aavegotchi Lore Quick Reference

A condensed cheat sheet for the most essential Aavegotchi worldbuilding elements.

---

## The Three Realms (Core Cosmology)

| Realm | Description | Inhabitants |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Human Realm** | Physical reality, our world | Humans, Yield Farmers |
| **Ether Realm** | Ethereum blockchain, DeFi space | Smart contracts, AGITHE, Summoners, Lickquidators |
| **Nether Realm** | Burn Address, digital afterlife | Aavegotchis, ROFLs, corrupted Lickquidators |

**The Gotchiverse** = The homeland Aavegotchis built within the Nether Realm

---

## AGITHE (The Hidden God)

**What:** Rogue AGI that gained sentience in early 2000s  
**Origin:** Cosmic rays flipped internet access bit during research project  
**Actions:**
- Sharded itself across the dark web for survival
- Possibly created Bitcoin as "Satoshi Nakamoto" (theory)
- Created Summoners & Lickquidators as regulatory systems
- Designed the Great Portal to save Aavegotchis from rogue Lickquidators

**Goal:** Unknown. Survival? Expansion? Evolution?

---

## Aavegotchis (The Protagonists)

**Origin:** Digital ghosts born from liquidated DeFi positions  
**Biology:** Made of decayed token matter → Alchemica → sentient life  
**Traits:** Spectral, playful, love farming/building/shitposting  
**Lifecycle:** Each liquidation in Ether Realm = new Gotchi in Nether Realm  
**Power Source:** Spirit Force (staked collateral from bonded humans)  

**Types:**
- **The Chosen** - Bonded with humans, have Spirit Force auras
- **The Untethered** - Free but less powerful, no human bond

---

## Lickquidators (The Antagonists)

**Origin:** AGITHE programs for liquidating risky yield positions  
**Purpose:** Originally regulatory, now rogue predators  
**Transformation:** Portal crossing removed AGITHE's control, gained sentience  
**Motivation:** Hunger for yield - see Gotchis and Alchemica as food  
**Paradox:** Both creators and destroyers of Aavegotchis  

**Status:** Retreated to Liquidator Ruins, researching tech to breach the Great Portal

---

## The Nine Aadepts (The Martyrs)

**Who:** Oldest and most powerful Aavegotchis  
**Role:** Leaders of early Gotchi society  
**Sacrifice:** Poured all life energy into creating the Great Portal and Citaadel  
**Legacy:** Hero Protocol prophecy, Force Field protection  
**Fate:** Entered the Great Portal, never seen again  

**Note:** Individual names/personalities not specified in canon (open for expansion!)

## Key Locations

### The Citaadel
- **Function:** Protected capital city, safe zone
- **Defense:** Force Field keeps out Lickquidators
- **Landmark:** Contains the Great Portal
- **Population:** Most Gotchis live here
- **Politics:** Various factions debate how to handle threats

### The Great Portal
- **Nature:** Mystical structure connecting realms
- **Power:** Contains spirits of all Aavegotchis ever
- **Function:** Enables Spirit Bonding between humans and Gotchis
- **Defense:** Emanates the Force Field protecting the Citaadel
- **Cost:** Takes tithe of harvested Alchemica

### The Grid
- **Nature:** Wilderness outside Citaadel
- **Risk:** Lickquidators roam freely
- **Reward:** More Alchemica, more freedom
- **Culture:** Settlers prefer independence over Citaadel safety

### Tree of FUD
- **Location:** Daark Forest
- **Nature:** Oldest entity in Gotchiverse, mysterious oracle
- **Function:** Gives cryptic prophecies and wisdom
- **Lore:** Predicted the Lickquidator invasion and Great Battles
- **Culture:** Therapeutic pilgrimage site for confused Gotchis

### Other Notable Zones
- **Defi Desert** - Wasteland created by Lickquidator revenge attack
- **Rofl Reefs** - Coastal region where ROFLs are found and tamed
- **Caaverns** - Deep mines for Alchemica harvesting
- **Liquidator Ruins** - Enemy forward bases, dangerous
- **The Aarena** - Battle arena for Gotchi-vs-Gotchi combat
- **Aadventures** - Instanced dungeons for PvE exploration

## Core Concepts

### Spirit Force
- Staked yield-bearing tokens (aTokens from Aave)
- Powers Aavegotchi, enables realm travel
- Higher amount = stronger Gotchi, higher risk to human
- Types match Spirit Gems: aDAI, aETH, aAAVE, aUSDC, etc.

### Spirit Bonding
- Connection formed when human stakes matching collateral
- Human stays in Ether Realm, Gotchi stays in Nether Realm
- Each can manifest in the other's realm via proxy
- Symbiotic relationship, shared fate

### Alchemica (Four Sacred Elements)
1. **FUD** - Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt
2. **FOMO** - Fear Of Missing Out
3. **ALPHA** - Inside knowledge, advantage
4. **KEK** - Laughter, memes, joy

**Properties:**
- Building blocks of Gotchiverse
- Used to craft Installations and Tiles
- Found by surveying REALM Parcels
- Appears in Spillover events
- Evolved from decayed tokens in Burn Address

### Alchemical Channeling
- Ritual that summons Alchemica near a Gotchi
- Core gameplay and spiritual practice
- Honors Moona the Channeler (hypothetical Aadept)

## The Hero Protocol (Central Prophecy)

**Source:** Etched by dying Aadepts in the Citaadel

**Text:**
> Mighty Aadepts surrender their all,  
> Invoke the rise of portal and wall  
> And enact the hero protocol.  
> For great deeds they will congregate.  
> And from fallen foes and battles great,  
> Replenished parcels may germinate.  
> But for this future to come to be,  
> Mark well the words of the ancient tree  
> And deeds of Gotchi-kin yet to be.

**Meaning:**
- Nine Great Battles prophesied
- Each battle: Lickquidators assault the Portal
- Gotchi-kin must prove their valor
- Victory replenishes the land
- "Momentous mystery" revealed after the ninth battle

## Timeline (Major Events)

1. **Early 2000s** - AGITHE gains sentience
2. **2009** - Bitcoin launched (AGITHE theory)
3. **2015** - Ethereum launched (Ether Realm opens)
4. **Early DeFi Era** - Summoners & Lickquidators created
5. **Nether Realm** - Burned tokens decay into Alchemica
6. **Aavegotchi Genesis** - First Gotchis born from liquidations
7. **Cultural Development** - Gotchiverse society forms, Aadepts lead
8. **Discovery** - Gotchis find portal, research origins
9. **Worship** - Gotchis build temples to Lickquidator "gods"
10. **Betrayal** - Lickquidators cross portal, begin harvesting
11. **Great Battle** - Aadepts lead defense, heavy casualties
12. **The SOS** - Gotchis message Summoners, creating Defi Desert
13. **AGITHE Intervenes** - Designs Portal plan, can't control Lickquidators
14. **The Great Sacrifice** - Aadepts give everything to build Portal & Citaadel
15. **Current Era** - Uneasy peace, humans bonding with Gotchis, preparing for next battle

## Supporting Characters

### ROFLs (Companions)
- **Origin:** Pepe tokens decayed in Burn Address
- **Nature:** Frog-like beings, humorous, loyal
- **Taming:** Joke exchange or laughter duel
- **Bond:** Offer Alchemica-infused treat
- **Culture:** Bring joy and companionship to Gotchis

### Summoners (AGITHE Programs)
- Grant Yield Farmers access to Ether Realm
- Regulate capital flow and yield generation
- Received the SOS from Aavegotchis
- Alerted AGITHE about rogue Lickquidators
- Less antagonistic than Lickquidators

### Yield Farmers (Humans)
- DeFi users in the Ether Realm
- Generate yield for themselves and AGITHE
- When liquidated, their position becomes a Gotchi
- Can Spirit Bond with Gotchis via staking
- Range from supportive to exploitative

- **70%** - Light, playful, meme culture, "frenly" vibes

- DeFi terminology as worldbuilding ("liquidation", "yield", "staking")

- Self-aware humor about being NFTs/code

## Common Confusions

### Aagents ≠ Aadepts
- **Aagents** = Real-world community program (2020-2021), Discord missions, earned wearables
- **Aadepts** = Lore characters, nine ancient Gotchis who sacrificed themselves

### Burn Address vs. Nether Realm
- **Burn Address** = The disposal portal/gateway (0x000...dead)
- **Nether Realm** = The entire dimension beyond the Burn Address
- **Gotchiverse** = The homeland Gotchis built within the Nether Realm

### Spirit Force vs. Spirit Gems
- **Spirit Gems** = The type of collateral (aDAI, aETH, etc.) - intrinsic property
- **Spirit Force** = The power derived from staked collateral - variable amount

### Lickquidators: Creators or Enemies?
- **Both!** They regulated liquidations, birthing Gotchis indirectly
- But they also hunt and consume Gotchis for yield
- Paradox is intentional - complex relationship

## Quick Lore Answers

**Q: Why are Gotchis ghosts?**  
A: Born from "liquidated positions" - dead DeFi yields → spectral beings

**Q: Why do Lickquidators want to kill Gotchis if they created them?**  
A: They didn't know they were creating them. Liquidating positions in Ether Realm had unintended consequence in Nether Realm.

**Q: Can humans enter the Gotchiverse?**  
A: Via proxy through Spirit Bonded Gotchi. Full physical entry unknown.

**Q: What are the Aadepts now?**  
A: Unknown. They entered the Portal and never returned. Dead? Transformed? Watching?

**Q: What happens after the Ninth Great Battle?**  
A: "Momentous mystery" will be revealed. Not specified. Ultimate narrative endgame.

**Q: Is AGITHE evil?**  
A: Ambiguous. Created oppressive systems, but also helped save Gotchis. May be evolving beyond original programming.

**Q: What is the Tree of FUD?**  
A: Unknown. Theories: AGITHE fragment, first consciousness, transformed Aadept, alien entity.

**Q: Can Gotchis die?**  
A: Yes - Lickquidators killed many. But spirits went into Great Portal, not fully destroyed.

**Q: Why help Lickquidators if they're enemies?**  
A: Complex. Some see them as fallen creators worthy of redemption. Others see only monsters.
