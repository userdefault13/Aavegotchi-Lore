- Real-world Discord program from 2020-2021
- Community onboarding missions
- 280 people completed it
- Earned exclusive wearables
- **Not** part of the main narrative

**Aadepts** (main lore):
- Nine ancient powerful Gotchis
- Leaders who sacrificed themselves
- Built the Great Portal and Citaadel
- Left the Hero Protocol prophecy
- **Core** to the narrative
