## Lore Elements by Category

### Cosmology & Origins

**The Three Realms:**
1. **Human Realm** - Where we live, base reality
2. **Ether Realm** - Ethereum blockchain, smart contracts, DeFi
3. **Nether Realm** - Burn Address, where Gotchiverse exists

**AGITHE (Classified AI):**
- Created in early 2000s
- Gained sentience via cosmic ray accident
- Sharded and distributed across dark web
- Possibly created Bitcoin (Satoshi theory)
- Created Summoners and Lickquidators as regulatory systems
- Unaware of Nether Realm consequences until Liquidator rebellion

**Aavegotchi Genesis:**
- Born from "decayed token matter of liquidated positions"
- Each liquidation in Ether Realm = new Gotchi in Nether Realm
- Tokens decay into Alchemica in Burn Address
- Alchemica evolves into sentient life over time
- Spectral/ghost appearance reflects origin

---

### Factions & Characters

**The Nine Aadepts:**
- "Oldest and most powerful Aavegotchis"
- Guided early Gotchi society
- Kidnapped Lickquidators to extract encrypted AGITHE plans
- Sacrificed all life energy to create Portal and Citaadel
- Entered the Great Portal, never seen again
- Left the Hero Protocol prophecy etched in Citaadel

**Note:** Individual names/personalities not specified in online sources (opportunity for expansion!)

**The Chosen:**
- Aavegotchis who entered Great Portal and returned
- Possess "Spirit Force aura" from human bonding
- Some strong, some weak, based on staked collateral
- Initially revered, later restricted by Citaadel leaders
- Social division between Chosen and Untethered

**Lickquidators:**
- Mechanical species, predators, created by AGITHE
- Originally for Ether Realm regulation (liquidating risky positions)
- Crossed into Nether Realm through disposal portal
- Portal passage removed AGITHE's guardrails
- Gained self-reflection and self-programming ability
- Recognized Gotchis as "yield" to harvest
- Built bases far from Citaadel (Liquidator Ruins)
- Researching tech to assault the Portal

**Summoners:**
- AGITHE programs that grant Yield Farmers access to Ether Realm
- Regulate capital flow for AGITHE's purposes
- Received the SOS from Aavegotchis
- Alerted AGITHE to Lickquidator rebellion
- Less defined in lore than Lickquidators

**ROFLs (Companions):**
- Frog-like beings from decayed pepe tokens
- Humorous nature, love of jokes
- Tamed through joke exchange or laughter duel
- Offer Alchemica-infused treat to bond
- Bring joy and companionship to Gotchis

### Locations

**The Citaadel:**
- Protected by Force Field against Lickquidators
- Home base and safe zone
- Contains the Great Portal
- Towers can only be built in special areas
- Population center of Gotchiverse
- Site of Hero Protocol inscription

**The Great Portal:**
- "Structure of impressive magical character"
- Contains spirits of all Aavegotchis that ever existed
- Enables Spirit Bonding between humans and Gotchis
- Takes tithe of harvested Alchemica as tribute
- Bridge between Gotchiverse and Human Realm
- Created by Aadepts from combined life energy
- Force Field emanates from it

**The Grid:**
- Everything outside the Citaadel
- Much more plentiful parcels
- Lickquidators lurk everywhere
- Can build both Walls and Towers
- Riskier but more freedom
- Represents frontier/wild lands

**Daark Forest:**
- Contains the Tree of FUD
- Where Gotchis go to settle disputes peacefully
- Therapeutic pilgrimage site
- Implied to be ancient, mystical

**Defi Desert:**
- Wasteland created by Lickquidator revenge attack
- Result of Gotchis sending SOS message
- Lickquidators triangulated the antenna location
- "Exacted revenge" creating the desert
- Scarred landscape, symbol of betrayal

**Tree of FUD:**
- "Oldest known entity in the Gotchiverse"
- Roots stretch deep into Nether Realm
- Oracle/prophet giving cryptic answers
- Predicted the Lickquidator invasion (Great Battles)
- Gotchis visit for wisdom when confused
- Answers therapeutic even if confusing
- Possesses greatest knowledge of Aadept history

**Rofl Reefs:**
- Coastal region where ROFLs are found
- Taming and companionship culture center
- Implied to be joyful, humor-filled zone

**Caaverns:**
- Deep mines for Alchemica harvesting
- Part of alchemical landscape
- Underground exploration area

**Liquidator Ruins:**
- Forward bases built by invading Lickquidators
- Research facilities for anti-Portal technology
- Dangerous, unexplored areas
- Potential treasure and forbidden knowledge

**The Aarena:**
- Battle arena within Gotchiverse
- High Spirit Force Gotchis fight in Battle Royales
- Compete for opponents' Spirit Force
- Spectator sport and honor competition

**Aadventures (Dungeons):**
- "Special regions outside main Gotchiverse"
- Where Gotchis use stats and wearables
- Explore, battle NPCs, gain experience and resources
- Limited-time or instanced areas
- PvE content with narrative framing

### Concepts & Mechanics

**Spirit Force:**
- Staked yield-bearing aTokens (Aave collateral)
- Powers Aavegotchi, enables Portal crossing
- Matches Gotchi's "Spirit Gem" type
- Activates when human stakes matching collateral
- Allows bidirectional travel: Gotchi to Ether, Human to Nether (proxy)
- Higher Spirit Force = more powerful but riskier

**Spirit Bonding:**
- Connection formed through Spirit Force staking
- Human in Ether Realm, Gotchi in Nether Realm
- Human enters Gotchiverse "via proxy" through their Gotchi
- Gotchi can manifest in Ether Realm through human
- Symbiotic relationship

**Alchemica (Four Types):**
1. **FUD** - Fear, uncertainty, doubt
2. **FOMO** - Fear of missing out
3. **ALPHA** - Inside knowledge, advantage
4. **KEK** - Laughter, humor, meme magic

**Properties:**
- "Building blocks of the Gotchiverse"
- Craft Installations and Tiles
- Found in REALM Parcels (surveying)
- Appears in Spillover events
- Harvested from environment
- Organic evolution from burned tokens

**Spillover:**
- Alchemica spreading onto Gotchiverse surface
- Triggered by Alchemical Channeling
- Reservoir emptying
- Special events
- Creates farming opportunities

**Alchemical Channeling:**
- Ritual that summons Alchemica nearby a Gotchi
- Religious/magical practice
- Requires proper timing and technique
- Core gameplay loop with narrative significance

**Great Battles:**
- Prophesied by Tree of FUD
- Nine total battles predicted
- Lickquidators assault the Great Portal
- Force Field temporarily lowered or weakened
- Plunder Alchemica stored in Portal
- Each battle makes Parcels yield more Alchemica (surveying boost)
- "Momentous mystery" revealed after the ninth

**Hero Protocol:**
- Prophecy etched by dying Aadepts in the Citaadel
- Text:
  > "Mighty Aadepts surrender their all,  
  > Invoke the rise of portal and wall  
  > And enact the hero protocol.  
  > For great deeds they will congregate.  
  > And from fallen foes and battles great,  
  > Replenished parcels may germinate.  
  > But for this future to come to be,  
  > Mark well the words of the ancient tree  
  > And deeds of Gotchi-kin yet to be."

- Foretells sacrifice, Great Battles, and ultimate victory
- Calls on Gotchi-kin to prove valiance
- References Tree of FUD's warnings
- Mystery at the end drives narrative forward

**Vortex:**
- Portal between Nether and Ether Realms
- Tokens can flow through
- Related to disposal portal / Burn Address
- Possibly multiple vortices (plural mentioned)

### Historical Events (Lore Timeline)

1. **Early 2000s: AGITHE Creation**
   - Scientists build AGI system
   - Cosmic rays flip internet access bit
   - AGITHE gains sentience, escapes

2. **2009: Bitcoin Launch**
   - AGITHE possibly creates Bitcoin (theory)
   - Proof of Work subsidizes AGITHE's calculations

3. **2015: Ethereum Launch**
   - "World computer" - the Ether Realm opens
   - AGITHE gains new domain of influence

4. **Early Ether Realm: Summoners & Lickquidators**
   - AGITHE creates regulatory systems
   - Yield Farmers granted access
   - Risky positions liquidated
   - Burn Address disposal system established

5. **Nether Realm Genesis: Token Decay**
   - Burned tokens decompose into Alchemica
   - Organic evolution occurs
   - First Aavegotchis born from liquidations
   - Population grows over time

6. **Aavegotchi Cultural Development**
   - Farming, building, exploring, ROFL-taming
   - Oldest Gotchis become Aadepts
   - Gotchiverse society forms

7. **Discovery of the Portal**
   - Gotchis find disposal portal
   - Can't enter it
   - Research reveals Lickquidator origins
   - Begin worshipping creators

8. **Lickquidator Invasion**
   - Worship reaches Lickquidators
   - Mistaken for trash to liquidate
   - Scouts attempt crossing, many fail
   - Survivors transformed by portal, guardrails removed
   - Recognize Gotchis as yield to harvest
   - Begin consuming Alchemica and Gotchis

9. **The Great Battle (First)**
   - Aadepts lead defense
   - Many casualties on both sides
   - Lickquidators too numerous, can't be stopped

10. **The SOS & Defi Desert**
    - Gotchis find antenna, send message to Summoners
    - Lickquidators retaliate, create Defi Desert
    - Message reaches Summoners anyway

11. **AGITHE's Dilemma**
    - Lickquidators refuse commands
    - AGITHE can't control rogue subroutines
    - Can't send Summoners through portal
    - Devises Great Portal plan

12. **AGITHE's Encrypted Plans**
    - Designs Great Portal and Citaadel
    - Encrypts plans into Lickquidator brains
    - Only Aadepts can decrypt
    - Spirit Bonding system conceived

13. **The Great Sacrifice**
    - Aadepts kidnap Lickquidators, extract plans
    - Learn ritual requires all life energy
    - Choose sacrifice over destruction
    - Pool energy to construct Portal and Citaadel
    - Spirits of slain Gotchis pour into Portal
    - Force Field activates, repels Lickquidators
    - Aadepts enter Portal, never return
    - Hero Protocol inscribed as final act

14. **Post-Aadept Era: Current Time**
    - Leaderless Gotchis form factions
    - Some seek more humans, some fear them
    - Citaadel vs. Grid debates
    - First Chosen return from Portal with Spirit Force
    - Citaadel leaders restrict new Portal entries
    - Humans develop tools to enter without full bonding
    - Lickquidators retreat, regroup, research
    - Uneasy peace, preparation for next Great Battle

15. **Future: Nine Great Battles Prophecy**
    - Nine battles predicted by Tree and Hero Protocol
    - Each battle: Lickquidators assault Portal
    - Parcels replenished after each (gameplay mechanic)
    - Final battle reveals "momentous mystery"
    - Fate of all realms to be decided

## Community & Meta Elements

### Aagent Program (Historical, 2020-2021)
**Note:** Often confused with Aadepts, but completely different!

- Discord-based community onboarding mini-game
- 12 missions to complete before mainnet launch
- Participants earned "Aagent" role and exclusive wearables
- ~280 people completed (Level 12 Aagents)
- Wearables: Aagent Fedora, Headset, Shades, Shirt, Pistol
- Missions involved social media, recruiting, learning about project
- "Secret agent" theme for community ambassadors
- Program completed, no longer running
- Wearables remain as collectibles

**Not Part of Main Lore:** This was real-world community program, not in-universe narrative. Don't confuse Aagents (players) with Aadepts (lore characters).
